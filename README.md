 📊 Project Overview
This data analytics project focuses on evaluating global retail performance for a confectionery enterprise. By processing transactional data, the analysis uncovers critical trends in revenue growth, purchasing habits, geographic distribution, and product performance to drive strategic decision-making.

💡 Key Business Insights Captured
Growth Trajectory: A comprehensive time-series analysis mapping revenue from 2022 through late 2024, highlighting seasonal sales spikes and operational consistency.

Geographic Breakdown: Evaluation of the Average Ticket value across key international markets, revealing that the UK and USA lead in high-value customer transactions.

Weekly Performance Metrics: An operational breakdown showing that Thursday and Friday generate peak transaction volumes, offering data-backed windows for targeted marketing campaigns.

Product Portfolio Analysis: A dual-focused look at product success, identifying "Smooth Sliky Salty" as both the top revenue generator ($1,120,201) and a major share of total volume via an interactive product mix donut chart.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
Data Manipulation & Processing: Pandas, NumPy

Data Visualization & Dashboard UI: Matplotlib, Seaborn, GridSpec

Design Principles: Advanced custom theme styling, cohesive dark-mode UI design, and optimized information hierarchy for executive-level readability.
